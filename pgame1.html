Plantformer

I wanted to make a platformer game so I wanted to call my project platformer, but misspelt it to make which led to the name plantformer.

This game was the first time I made anything with pixel art, sprites, and honestly unity and game making in general. So this was a great learning experience and honestly a decent game for the skills that I had at the time.

Player

These are the animations that I made for the player character of the game. As you can see on the side here, it is an ant and it moves.

Enemies

All the organisms that you see on the right can and will kill this ant that your player character is. The cactus is used just like spikes in other platformers. The evil ant walks around and is slightly faster than your ant. And the flesh plant just eats you. The fleshy plant also eats you, but you can jump on top of it without dying.

Goals

There are two kinds of goals in the game. One of them is just the normal goal of getting to the end of the level. The other is getting all the strawberries in the level. These can also be seen on the right.
